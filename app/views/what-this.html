The possible future of HAS

What is this?

For a long time we've struggled to get a clear picture of what HAS will be in our heads. Vision documents and design principles get us a certain distance but not all the way.

It often feels like we mostly agree on where we're going, but without something to point at we don't know that we agree.

On a couple of occasions, in an attempt to explain what I'm describing of the future of HAS, I've taken to saving local copies of prototypes and hacking the HTML to do what I want. Over time, this has grown up into something that is almost a coherent service.

Therefore these screens are an attempt to draw out some of what the future service might look like. It doesn't cover everything, the designs are wonky (I'm not a designer!) and lots of the links are broken. Also, there's no interactivity. But hopefully, they might just give a flavour of where we're going
